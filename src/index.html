About   Hi! I'm woflydev. I come from the land down under, and currently reside in Taipei Tokyo Brisbane. Being an avid roboticist, software engineer, and aspiring law student, I co-founded non-profit organization Theseus Robotics in 2024 to give other students like myself the opportunity to excel in competitive robotics. In the past, I've been the first ever high-school student to win QUT's Droid Racing Challenge, an autonomous university-level car racing competition.
